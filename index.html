Frequently Asked Questions

To order online at foodpanda, follow these steps:

  • Go to the foodpanda website or app.
  • Enter your delivery address.
  • Browse through the list of restaurants and choose the one you want to order from.
  • Add the items you want to your cart.
  • Review your order and checkout.

No, you do not need to create a foodpanda account to place an order. However, creating an account will make it easier to reorder your favorite food, track your orders, and save your payment

Delivery time varies depending on the restaurant, the time of day, and the distance between the restaurant and your delivery address. However, you can see an estimated delivery time for your order.

Delivery costs vary depending on the restaurant and the distance between the restaurant and your delivery address. You can see the delivery cost for your order before you checkout.

You can pay for your foodpanda order with a variety of payment methods, including cash, credit card, debit card, and mobile wallets.
